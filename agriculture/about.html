About Us

About Us

India's 60-70% of the populatiopn is directly or indirectly dependent on agriculture. However, there is ever increasing pressure on farming sector to deliver more per unit of land because:

  • Lesser availability of arable land due to massive urbanization and expansion of cities.
  • A huge population of ~1.25 Billion increasing at a rate of 1.2% per year.
  • Majority (70%) of farming dependent on seasonal rains.
  • Massive increase in input costs compared to farm output selling prices.
  • Lack of technical knowledge & ways to enhance farm productivity.

Our Story

It is a story of friends who were raised in typical middle income household of service class society of India of 70's & 80's. They witnessed idealistic parenting and schooling. They went to college together and majored in Agriculture from College of Agriculture. Both had biology as their major in high school. In first year of college they were roommates. Both had a great time with participation in almost all the academics and extracuricular activities. After graduation all of them took different roads for jobs and careers. Vinit started his own landscape & horticulture consulatncy firm (PRAKRATI) in partnership with Paresh Sharma (currently in Dubai working with a landscaping company). Raj joined an agrochemical company (Hoechst India Ltd.) and worked as a marketing & sales officer.Times passed by everyone moved on with their initial job, worked at several places, started family, and estbalished themselves very well in their lives and society. About 20 years later one of the friends Sameer Agrawal started a Whatsapp group of their batch and connected almost 40 of these old friends. After initial catching up, they realized that nothing has changed in some of them, the same enthusiasm to do something different, the passion to solve the problems, the ideology about the "real application" of their education, the zeal to give back to the society and country that educated them, nurtured them, made them stand on their feets was the same. It was same "fire" in each one of them and quite disturbed by the detoriating conditions of marginal farmers, farmer suicide graph going up, farmers not getting right comensation for the produce etc. Based on their experience and struggles in life they knew that t government subsidies and loan waivers are only temprory solutions. The real solution is to make farmers self sufficient, provide technical know-how which they can practically adopt, porvide them ways to add value to their produce and provide access to endusers directly so that their profits are not stolen by middelmen and speculators. Inspired with this these thoughts and self-realization that they can make definitely make a difference with their expertise a company named Indo AmericanAgsciences was formed.

OUR MISSION

  • To provide farmers technical know-hows to enhance their productivity and overall health of farmig operations.
  • To educate farmers to minimize their input expenses by introducing them to alternate ways of farming.
  • To provide farmers high quality inputs such as biopesticides, biofertilizers, and other latest technologies.
  • To provide farmers a direct supply chain up to the endusers to increase their profitability.
  • To connect end users highest quality oof produces with "zero" or "no" chemical pesticides used.

OUR VISION

  • Increase farmer's net income per acre of land so that quality of life improves for farm families.
  • Increase number of jobs in rural areas by establishing direct supply chains and processing units close to farms.
  • Increase the availability of food produced organically or with "zero" pesticide usage to urban populations.
  • Increase the availability of unadultrated food and milk products to urban populations and increase their awareness towards the healthy foods.
  • Enhance the connection between farmers and the endusers for betterment of the society in longer run.
